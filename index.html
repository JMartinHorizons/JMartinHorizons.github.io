James Martin

Artist CV

James Martin lives, works and writes in Melbourne, Australia. His recent writing has been focused on digital literature, including Twine works and digital poetry. Otherwise, his interests are in food writing and the many ways it intersects with life. His work has been published in the Bowen Street Press Review.
